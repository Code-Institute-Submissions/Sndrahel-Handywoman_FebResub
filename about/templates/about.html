This is us

We have connected more than 500 corporate women and female entrepreneurs, and evolved the name to Handywoman, where women are still the majority, but men feel welcome and comfortable.

What we do

We network and connect women with other women. We do everything from small to big jobs such as: making and repairing things in their houses, construction, mechanics, law, logistic, webb and many other services.

Why we care

We want to make a change in services where men is largely dominated. We aim to build circles of trust with one another because we may be experiencing similar hurdles. We belive that the way to overcome some of these hurdles is to form close connections with other women, who can share experiences from women who have been there and done that.
